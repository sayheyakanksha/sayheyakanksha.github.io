Designing for inclusion, always enjoying RPG adventures and forever a metalhead.

Growing up in India as a first-generation college student from a marginalized community, I learned early on how systems aren’t always designed for everyone. Even something as simple as not having a last name exposed me to bureaucratic challenges and social exclusion. These experiences shaped my passion for building inclusive, user-centered products—because I know firsthand how frustrating it is to feel like an afterthought in design and technology.


I started my career as a software engineer, but I quickly realized that I was most drawn to understanding users and shaping meaningful product experiences. Over the years, I’ve worked as a product manager for digital content and e-commerce platforms, leading product strategies that balance business goals with real human needs.


Now, as I complete my Master’s in Human-Computer Interaction, I’m focused on creating digital experiences that feel seamless, accessible, and impactful.


If you’d like to chat or work together, feel free to reach out at sayheyakanksha@gmail.com — let’s connect!
