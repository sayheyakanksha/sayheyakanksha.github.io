Problem Statement

The Challenge of Scaling Discoverability on Nykaa Fashion

Nykaa Fashion is a fashion and lifestyle e-commerce platform offering a curated mix of brands and styles to users. As the platform scaled, the growing number of brands and product categories made it challenging to maintain discoverability and visibility, impacting the user experience and business outcomes.


I led a cross-functional team of engineers and UI designers across web and mobile platforms to create an intuitive navigation experience that balanced user needs with business goals, ensuring seamless exploration of the online store.

User needs vs business needs

User Research

Key Research Questions

To inform our design strategy, we explored the following questions:

Physical Shopping Navigation

How do users navigate through stores and discover products or brands in a physical shopping mall? What parallels can we draw for an e-commerce experience?

Information Organization

Using card sorting exercises, how would users group and organize the information available to them for easier discovery?

Planned vs. Impulsive Shoppers

How do the journeys of planned shoppers differ from those of impulsive buyers, and what features can support both user personas effectively?

Sketching user journey

Brainstorming how people navigate while shopping - in physical stores vs digital stores

DESIGN EXPLORATION

Aligning User Needs with Business Goals

We assessed the design flow from the user's mental model perspective and designed mockups that the existing information architecture could accommodate. We were in a continuous ideation loop with business and design teams, using the insights from user research.

Design Exploration

Evaluating user mental models and design flows - product discovery with intent is the key!

Final Designs

Crafting Scalable and User-Centric Navigation Experiences

We finalized the overall layout and organization of the product properties, as well as how they will be presented to users. We focused on keeping the layouts and user flow intuitive and easy-to-use.


We also came up with multiple layouts that could highlight different sections. In order to test and evaluate their efficacy, we evaluated each of them, based on usability testing and A/B experiments.
