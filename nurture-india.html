Lauching an e-commerce platform empowering local artisans.
Nurture India is a grassroots initiative supporting Indian artisans from underrepresented communities. Lacking an online presence to reach global markets, I designed and developed their e-commerce website, enabling them to expand their reach, share their stories, and grow their business on a scalable, user-friendly platform.
So we asked: how do we make it easy for users to discover brands and products on Nykaa Fashion?

Understanding the Goal

I worked closely with Somya (Founder) to understand what the artisans needed and what customers might want when buying handcrafted products online.

Designing the Experience

The website design was clean and simple, with a focus on the artisans’ work and stories. I wanted their creativity to be the star.

Building the Platform

I set up the e-commerce site using Shopify, making sure it was easy to navigate, manage orders, and handle payments.

Integrating Data Analytics

To help the team understand customer behavior and track sales performance, I integrated analytics tools that provided insights into traffic, popular products, and customer trends.

Mailchimp for Outreach

I set up Mailchimp integration to help the team send personalized updates and promotions to their growing customer base, ensuring better engagement and repeat purchases.

Empowering the Team

I trained Somya’s team to confidently manage the website, use the analytics tools, and run email campaigns so they could keep the platform running smoothly and independently after launch.
