Overview

Helping Artisans Reach a Global Audience

Nurture India, founded by Somya Suresh, is all about uplifting artisans from underrepresented communities in India. They create beautiful handicrafts but often struggle to sell their work beyond local markets. The initiative works with artisans to sell their handicrafts online, sharing profits directly with the creators and helping them reach a wider audience.

Problem

Artisans Deserve More Visibility


Indian artisans are incredibly skilled, but most lack access to global markets. They rely on local buyers, which limits their growth and income. Somya wanted to change this by taking their work online and reaching customers around the world, but she needed help building a platform to make it happen.

Solution

Building an Online Home for Handicrafts

I stepped in to design and build a simple yet impactful e-commerce website for Nurture India. The site focused on showcasing the artisans’ work, telling their stories, and making it easy for customers to support them. By creating a platform that was both functional and easy to manage, we gave these artisans the opportunity to sell their crafts to a global audience.

My Role

Designing the Digital Shift

  • Understanding the Goal: I worked closely with Somya to understand what the artisans needed and what customers might want when buying handcrafted products online.
  • Designing the Experience: The website design was clean and simple, with a focus on the artisans’ work and stories. I wanted their creativity to be the star.
  • Building the Platform: I set up the e-commerce site using Shopify, making sure it was easy to navigate, manage orders, and handle payments.
  • Integrating Data Analytics: To help the team understand customer behavior and track sales performance, I integrated analytics tools that provided insights into traffic, popular products, and customer trends.
  • Mailchimp for Outreach: I set up Mailchimp integration to help the team send personalized updates and promotions to their growing customer base, ensuring better engagement and repeat purchases.
  • Empowering the Team: I trained Somya’s team to confidently manage the website, use the analytics tools, and run email campaigns so they could keep the platform running smoothly and independently after launch.

What I learned
