Overview

Bringing Traditional Artisans to a Global Market

POPxo was started in 2017 as an Indian women-centric blog. By the year 2019, my team and I helped it grow into a one million-strong online community where women could share their experiences and seek advice on topics such as fashion, beauty, marriage, sex, and lifestyle. As a Product Manager at POPxo (2017 - 2020), I played a pivotal role in transforming the platform into a multifaceted digital powerhouse. My work spanned content optimization, e-commerce integration, product launches, and retail expansion, driving engagement, revenue, and brand recognition. I worked closely with cross-functional teams to ensure seamless delivery of projects that boosted user engagement and drove revenue.
