Overview

Bringing Traditional Artisans to a Global Market

Nykaa Fashion is a fashion and lifestyle e-commerce platform which provides a curated mix of brands and styles to users. As the business scaled, maintaining the discoverability and visibility of an increasing number of brands and product categories became a challenge.

Project Overview

Key Contributions

Led product design and development process, collaborating with designers and engineers, driving Agile development. Spearheaded customer beta and design partner programs to develop detailed personas, use cases and functional specifications in shipping features.

User Needs
  • Shopping Mall Visits: How do users navigate across different stores and discover products & brands in physical space?
  • Card Sorting: How would different users organize and group information available to them?
  • Planned vs Impulsive Buyers: How does the user journey of different user personas differ from each other?
Brand Needs
  • Ever Changing Collection: Constantly expanding brand collection and redefinition of categories demanded a highly flexible design.
  • Legacy Code: Outdated codebase for the application created additional hurdles in execution, data collection and analysis.
Overview

Building the MVP

We assessed the design flow from the user's mental model perspective and designed mockups that the existing information architecture could accommodate. We were in a continuous ideation loop with business and design teams, using the insights from user research.

Campaign Highlight: Cadbury’s #RealDosti

Plixxo made a sweet debut with Cadbury’s #RealDosti campaign on Friendship Day in India, and it remains one of the most memorable campaigns I worked on. This campaign celebrated true friendships and leveraged Plixxo’s network of influencers to spread the message through heartwarming stories and relatable content.

The #RealDosti campaign, powered by Plixxo, marked a highly successful debut for the platform. Collaborating with Cadbury, the campaign achieved impressive results:

  • 168 bloggers, 183 college ambassadors, and 100+ Instagram influencers and insiders participated, creating a massive wave of engagement.
  • Collectively, the campaign reached over 1,040,578 social media users, showcasing the power of influencer-driven marketing in India.
  • Strengthened Cadbury’s brand image through authentic, impactful storytelling on social media platforms.
