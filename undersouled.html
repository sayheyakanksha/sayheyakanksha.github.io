About Undersouled

Game Overview

This game was my project for the Intro to VR course, and it combines two of my all-time favorite games: Overcooked and Dark Souls. I wanted to create something fun yet challenging, blending the chaotic cooking gameplay of Overcooked with the dark, fantasy-inspired atmosphere of Dark Souls. The result? A unique level where players navigate a spooky, mystical kitchen to prepare dishes while dodging traps and solving challenges. It’s a little chaotic, a little creepy, and a whole lot of fun.


This game is set in a haunted dungeon kitchen filled with eerie lighting, ghostly characters, and fantasy-inspired props. You play as a little ghost chef, rushing to complete dishes while avoiding dangerous obstacles. Every aspect of the game reflects the dark, fantasy theme, from the environment to the sounds and animations.

What's in it?

Key Features

Environment and Materials

I used a mix of custom materials and lighting effects to give the kitchen a mysterious, otherworldly feel. The setting is detailed with low-poly dungeon elements and eerie decor.

Player Input and Camera

The player controls the ghost chef through smooth, responsive input. The camera follows the action using Unity’s Cinemachine, making it easy to focus on gameplay even in chaotic moments.

Interactions and Triggers

Players interact with objects like ingredients, cauldrons, and spooky dining tables. Some objects trigger unique events, like a flying bat swooping in to disrupt your work.
